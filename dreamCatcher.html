About us

Dream Catcher was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to help people find jobs. It is now the largest job website in the world, boasting 250 million monthly users with 10 new job listings added every second. Biggest does not always mean best, but we chose Indeed as the best overall job website due to its size, number of industries and lifestyles catered to, and its unmatched update frequency.
